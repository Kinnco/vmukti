CO-AUTHORING APPLICATION

VMukti provides facility of Co-Authoring. 
Co-Authoring allows multi user to write/edit 
something on same page collaboratively.
	
	How to Use:
	• Drag co-authoring widget on the tab. 
	• Drag Buddies on Co-Authoring from Buddy list. 
	• Activities on this Widget display to all the 
	  participants of the Co-Authoring.
