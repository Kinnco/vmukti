WHITEBOARD APPLICATION

White Board widget more or less have the functionalities 
of paint. User can draw circle, rectangle, line etc. on the
whiteboard. User also can see pictures on the white board.
	
	How to Use:
	• Drag White-Board widget on the tab. 
	• Add buddies in this widget to enable 
	  them to see the activities performed on whiteboard 

There is a single painting pad for User & their buddies 
i.e. whatever written by user will be visible to 
     their online buddies and vice-versa
